About EXOVISION

Our Mission

EXOVISION is an interactive exoplanet exploration platform designed to make the vast universe of discovered exoplanets accessible to everyone. We analyze data from NASA's Exoplanet Archive to provide detailed insights into over 4,000 confirmed exoplanets, their characteristics, and potential habitability.

The Team

EXOVISION was developed by five dedicated students from Army Public School Hyderabad Cantt for the NASA Space Apps Challenge 2025, hosted in Hyderabad, Pakistan.

Data Source

All exoplanet data is sourced from NASA Exoplanet Archive, maintained by the California Institute of Technology under contract with NASA.

API Endpoint:

https://exoplanetarchive.ipac.caltech.edu/TAP/sync?query=select+pl_name,pl_rade,pl_bmasse,pl_orbsmax,pl_eqt,st_teff,st_rad,pl_insol+from+pscomppars&format=csv

Features

  • Search and explore 4,000+ confirmed exoplanets
  • Detailed statistics: radius, mass, gravity, temperature, orbital period
  • Habitability analysis with composite scores
  • Rankings: most habitable, hottest, largest, most massive, and more
  • Side-by-side planet comparison
  • Random planet discovery with "Surprise Me" feature
  • Beautiful space-themed interface with video backgrounds
